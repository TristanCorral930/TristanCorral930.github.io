Computer Controlled Cutting

Vinyl Sticker Documentation

Design

I created the sticker design using vector software. The main challenge was simplifying the shapes so the cutter could process them cleanly.

Cutting

The vinyl cutter followed the vector paths and cut the design into adhesive vinyl. I monitored the machine to make sure the blade didn’t snag.

Weeding

Weeding involved removing the excess vinyl around the design. This step was delicate, especially around thin lines and small details.

Applying

I applied transfer tape and carefully placed the sticker onto a surface. Pressing down evenly ensured a smooth final application.

Mini-Reflection

The hardest part of vinyl cutting was weeding fine details without tearing the design. I solved this by using a weeding tool and working slowly under bright light.

Laser Cutting

Laser cutting allowed for precise cuts on materials like wood and acrylic. Documentation for this step will be expanded later with images and notes.
