Embedded Programming

This page is dedicated to my Computer Controlled Cutting project. Here, I detail the process, tools, designs, and outcomes of this fabrication technique.

Project Overview

Computer Controlled Cutting involves the use of laser cutters and vinyl cutters to precisely shape materials. In this project, I experimented with designing vector files and producing components for a larger assembly.

Reflection

Through this project, I learned about tolerances in laser cutting, vector preparation, and material limitations. The cyberpunk aesthetic was achieved by integrating glowing acrylic elements with layered cuts.
